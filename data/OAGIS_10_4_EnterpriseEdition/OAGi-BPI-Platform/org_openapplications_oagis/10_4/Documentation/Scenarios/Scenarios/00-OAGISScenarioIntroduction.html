Introduction

The OAGIS Scenarios included within depict sample integration scenarios that the content of the Open Applications Group Integration Specification (OAGIS) supports.

These scenarios are intended to be used as models for designing one's own solutions based on the content offered by OAGIS. They are not meant to be Standards as such and they are not meant to be used only as illustrated. These scenarios are also not meant to be machine executable today but they are designed to lead to the development of such capabilities in the future.

Per the OAGi license, you may use these example Scenarios as is and you may modify them with attribution for the specific needs of your organization and your integration requirements.
