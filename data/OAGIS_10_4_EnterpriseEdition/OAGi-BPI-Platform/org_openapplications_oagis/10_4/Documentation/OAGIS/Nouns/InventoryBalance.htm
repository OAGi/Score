Element: InventoryBalance

Name InventoryBalance
Type InventoryBalanceType
Nillable no
Abstract no
Documentation Includes all stocked items and primarily represents the quantities of each item by location. Other item-by-location information, such as serial numbers or lot numbers, can also be included. The use of this noun does not include basic item master data that is independent of location, such as item description and dimensions. All Quantity information will be updated as replacing the entire field, not incremented by the quantity
More information at: http://www.openapplications.org/oagis/10.
