Element: Payable

Name Payable
Type PayableType
Nillable no
Abstract no
Documentation Payable is a transaction that represents an invoice from a supplier. A payable is an open item, approved and ready for payment, in the Accounts Payable ledger. In some systems it may be called a voucher. Compare to PurchaseLedgerInvoice, which represents a not yet approved supplier invoice.
More information at: http://www.openapplications.org/oagis/10.
