Element: PriceList

Name PriceList
Type PriceListType
Nillable no
Abstract no
Documentation Defines a list of items with their base price, price breaks, discounts and qualifiers. For each item, price breaks can be defined, above which certain discounts or overriding prices might apply. Price breaks can be defined in volume or in dollar amount. Price list qualifiers specify for which catalog, customer and/or effective dates this price list applies.
More information at: http://www.openapplications.org/oagis/10.
