Element: InspectionOrder

Name InspectionOrder
Type InspectionOrderType
Nillable no
Abstract no
Documentation An inspection order requests an inspection of an item instance, or collection of items which are serially identified. The inspection order can be the source document if coming directly from a customer. It may also related to a supplier purchase order, a manufacturing work order/instruction, finish goods testing, or random sampling of inventory. InspectionOrder can be considered a request noun, with TestResults be the delayed response noun. TestResults document will refer back to a specific InspectionOrder. Unlike InspectDelivery, InspectionOrder does not contain any references to the TestResults, or RejectionDetails. If (UseCase::= ERP=> LIMS); then Sample/TestRequest may not be provided if the LIMS holds the sample plan, and creates the list of samples to be taken If (UseCase::= External Customers => LIMS) OR (UseCase::= LIMS=>Third Party Labs); then Sample/TestRequest will be required Location may be Grower/Farm/Field hierarchy Sample information will likely be derived off a SamplingPlan noun, which determines sample size, frequency, and specific tests to be performed. 10.4; added
More information at: http://www.openapplications.org/oagis/10.
