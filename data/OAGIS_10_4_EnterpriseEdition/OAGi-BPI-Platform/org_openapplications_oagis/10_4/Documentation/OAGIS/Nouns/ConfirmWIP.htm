Element: ConfirmWIP

Name ConfirmWIP
Type ConfirmWIPType
Nillable no
Abstract no
Documentation Work-in-Progress confirmation represents confirmation of the movement of WIP materials. The noun refers to general information about the entire WIP transaction, as well as line item detail about the specific WIP operation or routing step. This may apply to the movement of raw materials or finished products.
More information at: http://www.openapplications.org/oagis/10.
