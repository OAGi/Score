Element: InventoryCount

Name InventoryCount
Type InventoryCountType
Nillable no
Abstract no
Documentation InventoryCount represents the results of a physical inventory or cycle count of the actual on-hand quantities of each item in each location. Compare to the noun InventoryBalance, which represents system-maintained on-hand quantities.
More information at: http://www.openapplications.org/oagis/10.
