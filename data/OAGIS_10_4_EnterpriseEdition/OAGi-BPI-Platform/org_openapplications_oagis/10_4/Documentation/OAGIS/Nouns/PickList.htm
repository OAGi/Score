Element: PickList

Name PickList
Type PickListType
Nillable no
Abstract no
Documentation Picking List is a document that lists material to be retrieved ("picked") from various locations in a warehouse in order to fill a production order, sales order, or shipping order. A picking list includes general identifying information (header information), as well as line item details. Depending on the verb used, PickList may refer to header information only, or both header and detail information.
More information at: http://www.openapplications.org/oagis/10.
