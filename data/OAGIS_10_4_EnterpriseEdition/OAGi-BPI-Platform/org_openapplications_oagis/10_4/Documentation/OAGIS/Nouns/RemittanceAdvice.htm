Element: RemittanceAdvice

Name RemittanceAdvice
Type RemittanceAdviceType
Nillable no
Abstract no
Documentation The purpose of the RemittanceAdvice Business Object Document is to provides a detailed breakdown of a payment amount. It includes all credits, debits, discounts, adjustments taken on an invoice, and adjustment reason codes. A payment can attach a Remittance Advice Notification. Payment can be for products only, transportation, or other services.
More information at: http://www.openapplications.org/oagis/10.

The Process RemittanceAdvice sends the electronic form of a remittance advice document from a payer to a payee. It is usually executed when an outstanding accounts payable balance (credit or debit) exists.
More information at: http://www.openapplications.org/oagis/10.
