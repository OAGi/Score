Element: RecoverWIP

Name RecoverWIP
Type RecoverWIPType
Nillable no
Abstract no
Documentation RecoverWIP is used to notify a Manufacturing Application of the creation of usable production materials from material previously considered unsuitable for production use. This is most often likely to represent a return to production of scrap material.
More information at: http://www.openapplications.org/oagis/10.
