Element: ProductionPerformance

Name ProductionPerformance
Type ProductionPerformanceType
Nillable no
Abstract no
Documentation ProductionPerformance is generally used in the Process Manufacturing Industries to provide information about the the actual results of the Production run. The noun refers to general information about the entire Process Production transaction. This may apply to the movement of raw materials, finished products or by products.
More information at: http://www.openapplications.org/oagis/10.
