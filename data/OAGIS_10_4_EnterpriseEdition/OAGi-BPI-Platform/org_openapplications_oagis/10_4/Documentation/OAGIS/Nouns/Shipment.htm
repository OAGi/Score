Element: Shipment

Name Shipment
Type ShipmentType
Nillable no
Abstract no
Documentation A Shipment document identifies and describes a specific collection of goods to be transported by a carrier and delivered to one or more business partner destinations. A Shipment document represents the extent and content of "transportation work" to be done by the carrier. For transportation efficiency, a shipment document typically consolidates deliveries to multiple destinations within a certain geographic region and may provide carrier routing instructions to each delivery stop.
More information at: http://www.openapplications.org/oagis/10.
