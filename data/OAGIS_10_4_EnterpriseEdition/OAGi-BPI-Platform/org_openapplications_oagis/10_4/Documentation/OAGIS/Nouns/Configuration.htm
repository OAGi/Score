Element: Configuration

Name Configuration
Type ConfigurationType
Nillable no
Abstract no
Documentation A specific instance of a configurable product line that might consisit of items, software, services and other "bundles" combined in such a way as to represent a unique grouping of selections from a large and sometimes infinite listing of possible combinations of possible options. This differs from a catalog item in that a catalog item can sometimes itself be a specific offered configuration but in this case, the configuration is custom developed to meet specific, non-catalog requirements and/or buyer needs.
More information at: http://www.openapplications.org/oagis/10.
